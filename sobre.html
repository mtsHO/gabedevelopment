Gabriel V. Orlandelli

Hi, there!

I'm Gabe (Gabriel Vateka Orlandelli) and am currently filling two different roles in the Game Industry:

- Lead Game Designer at Major Games

- Senior Product Marketing Analyst at Hoplon

I've graduated with a BFA in International Relations with an emphasis in Marketing and Business from ESPM, in my home country, Brazil.

After working on a mobile games publisher start-up that was essential for my first Business and Marketing experiences in the game industry, I decided to finish the BFA and move to Orlando and obtain an M.S. in Game Design from Full Sail University, graduating in Oct 2014.

During this graduate degree, I worked on several game projects and consequently assumed the role of Lead Designer of Victim 146 after its successful Global Game Jam build, which provided the initial push to start evolving as a developer and in leadership as well. A few months later, I joined a few of my hard-working classmates and friends to found Major Games and work on BiT Evolution, which was released in under 6 months.

At Major Games, I was able to further develop skills in game design and development by working on several unique projects, allowing the experience to go beyond a single genre, theme, or engine. By collaborating with a small and talented team, was able to further develop more knowledge on other areas such as Production,hat can make the bridge between Game Design and Marketing.
